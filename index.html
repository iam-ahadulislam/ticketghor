About TicketGhor

TicketGhor.com is a ticket booking system designed to streamline the reservation process for buses and trains.

The need for digital solutions in daily life has increased significantly in the modern era. As an important element of urban mobility, public transportation needs creative ways to improve passenger experience and efficiency. Due to urbanization and automation, traditional ticket booking techniques like manual reservation systems and physical ticket counters are becoming less and less effective. These methods are less beneficial to current users since they frequently include lengthy wait times, connectivity problems, and the possibility of human error.
